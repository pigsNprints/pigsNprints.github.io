Katelyn Lloyd's Design Document

Purpose:

Pigs and Prints’ purpose is to showcase graphic t-shirt designs to acquiring customers. Our website will showcase specific designs to viewers who wish to inquire about the design in further detail. Our categories are designed to help viewers easily navigate the website to find what they are looking for. In the event that a viewer is unable to find their desired design, we have provided a Custom area for the view to submit design requests. Our mission is to provide each viewer with their graphic tee needs at the convenience of their home.

Audience:

The target audience for this webpage will primarily be women ranging in age from 14 to 60 plus. This does not limit men from visiting the website, however the content is generally designed for ladies. It does not matter where the viewer is from nor what they do for a living. The content does have limiting hobbies such as farm attire, football gear, and animals. However there is still broad categories that would appeal to anyone such as seasonal wear and quotes. I will still have a custom design function that will allow users to submit their own which will appeal to all audience members. Users must have the internet to view this site.

Needs Assessment:

To determine what viewers are interested in for a graphic t-shirt website, I polled a select group of individuals. All questions required a short answer response and were asked directly to the person in order to avoid bias.

Questions

  1. Why would you visit this website? (Provide a concrete example)
    7 out of 8 said they would visit to look at designs or create their own design. Reasons include
    • Looking for a specific theme
    • Ability to custom design
    • Look at current designs
  2. What content are you most interested in?
    Due to the broadness of this questions, individuals interpreted this questions differently. Here are their reasons:
    • Sassy t-shirt ideas. Wedding gifts. Dog mom. Coffee lovers.
    • Graphic t’s/unique team t’s /sweatshirts/varsity/baseball style shirts
    • Shirts, hats, koozies, cups, (specialized projects - like what you did for the wedding gift)
    • Glitter text, trending t shirt slogans, punny t shirts
    • Graphic t’s with all the cute sayings
    • Couple/Group T-shirt designs
    • The school I teach at.
    • I am most interested in your shirt designs and to see if what is being offered would fit my style/size
  3. When do you see yourself using the “Submit Your Own” section?
    5 out of 8 said they would use it for a party or event. Reasons included
    • Bachelorette parties
    • Family events or gatherings
    • Themed parties
  4. How do you see yourself using the website?
    6 out of 8 said for personal use and to order shirts. Other responses included
    • Submitting a design idea
    • Sharing the website with others
  5. What would entice you to continue visiting the website?
    4 out of 8 said new or unique designs. Reasons included
    • Quality products
    • New inventory
    • Being happy with other finished products
  6. What categories are you hoping to find?
    The following categories were mentioned multiple times
    • sports/school
    • Sorority
    • Seasonal
    • Family and farm

Content:

Based on my survey, I will have the following pages for my viewers to navigate:

  • Seasonal-This page will include designs that go with the current season. Currently, there will be fall, halloween, and Thanksgiving items.
  • Family & Farm- This page will contain designs that have family sayings. It will also contain anything farm related.
  • Game Day- This page will contain designs that all have something to do with sports and teams.
  • Sayings & Quotes- This page will contain designs that have fun sayings and quotes. These are the shirts that don't necessarily belong in the other categories.
  • Custom Design- This page will include a form for viewers to fill out to submit their request for a custom design. I will be able to contact them to confirm their request.
  • About Us- This page will contain information about Pigs & Prints. It will explain the name and give additional information to the users.
